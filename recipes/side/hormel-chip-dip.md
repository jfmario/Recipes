# Hormel Chip Dip

This baked dip combines cream cheese, canned chili, and shredded cheddar into a fast, crowd-friendly side for chips.

Use this as an 8-serving party side and serve it hot while the cheese is fully melted.

## Ingredients

* 16 oz cream cheese (2 standard 8 oz blocks), softened
* 30 oz canned chili (2 cans, 15 oz each)
* 3 cups shredded cheddar cheese (about 12 oz)
* 1 large bag sturdy tortilla chips, for serving

## Steps

* Preheat the oven to 350F and set out a 9x13-inch baking dish.
* Let the 16 oz cream cheese sit at room temperature for 20 to 30 minutes until spreadable, then spread all 16 oz cream cheese in an even layer across the bottom of the 9x13-inch baking dish.
* Spoon all 30 oz canned chili over the cream cheese and spread it into an even layer that fully covers the cream cheese.
* Sprinkle all 3 cups shredded cheddar cheese evenly over the chili, covering the surface from edge to edge.
* Bake at 350F for 30 minutes, until the dip is bubbling around the edges and the 3 cups shredded cheddar cheese is fully melted.
* Rest the baked dip for 5 minutes, then serve hot with 1 large bag sturdy tortilla chips.
