# Gravy

This is a quick stovetop gravy sized for 8 servings as a side component with a larger meal.

Use flour for a classic roux-based gravy, or use arrowroot for a gluten-light option.

## Ingredients

* 4 cups broth
* 5 tbsp butter
* 5 tbsp flour (or 3 tbsp plus 1 tsp arrowroot)
* 3/4 tsp poultry seasoning (optional)

## Steps

* Measure 4 cups broth and keep it nearby so you can add it gradually while whisking.
* Place a medium saucepan over medium heat and melt 5 tbsp butter until fully liquid but not browned, about 1 to 2 minutes.
* If using flour, whisk in 5 tbsp flour 1 tbsp at a time until smooth and cook for 1 to 2 minutes to remove the raw flour taste. If using arrowroot, whisk in 3 tbsp plus 1 tsp arrowroot and cook for about 30 seconds.
* Slowly pour in the 4 cups broth while whisking constantly, starting with small splashes for the first 1 cup, then adding the remaining broth in a steady stream once smooth.
* Continue cooking over medium heat, stirring and whisking often, until the gravy thickens enough to coat a spoon, about 4 to 6 minutes.
* Turn off the heat, then stir in 3/4 tsp poultry seasoning if using, plus 1/2 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp black pepper.
* Taste and adjust with more salt and pepper as needed, then serve hot.
