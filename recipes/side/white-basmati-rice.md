# White Basmati Rice

This is a neutral, non-seasoned basmati rice base intended to pair with mains or to be mixed with other components (such as meat, vegetables, or sauces) in recipes targeting 9 cups cooked rice.

## Ingredients

* 3 cups white basmati rice

## Steps

* Place 3 cups basmati rice in a large bowl. Rinse with cold water, swish with your hand, drain, and repeat until the water runs mostly clear.
* Add 6 cups water to a medium pot and bring it to a full boil over high heat.
* Stir the rinsed rice into the boiling water, then reduce heat to low to maintain a gentle simmer.
* Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and simmer for 18 minutes without lifting the lid.
* Remove the pot from the heat, uncover briefly to check doneness, then cover again and let the rice steam off heat for 5 minutes.
* Fluff the rice gently with a fork and serve, or spread it on a tray to cool if using it as a component in another dish.
