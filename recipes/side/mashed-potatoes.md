# Mashed Potatoes

These mashed potatoes are portioned as a side dish for 8 people when served with other mains and sides.

## Ingredients

* 3 lbs russet potatoes
* 3 garlic cloves
* 6 tbsp butter
* 3/4 cup whole milk
* 1/4 cup sour cream (optional)
* 3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese (optional)

## Steps

* Peel 3 lbs russet potatoes and cut them into 1 1/2-inch chunks so they cook evenly.
* Place the potatoes in a large pot with 3 garlic cloves, then add enough cold water to cover by about 1 inch. Add 1 tbsp kosher salt to the water.
* Bring the pot to a boil over high heat, then reduce to a steady simmer and cook uncovered for 14 to 16 minutes, until the potatoes are fork-tender.
* While the potatoes cook, warm 3/4 cup whole milk in a small saucepan over low heat until hot but not boiling, about 2 to 3 minutes.
* Drain the potatoes and garlic well, then return them to the warm pot for 1 minute over low heat, shaking the pot to evaporate excess moisture.
* Add 6 tbsp butter to the potatoes and mash until mostly smooth.
* Pour in the warm 3/4 cup milk and continue mashing until creamy. For richer potatoes, mash in 1/4 cup sour cream.
* If using cheese, fold in 3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese until melted.
* Season with 1 tsp kosher salt and 1/2 tsp black pepper, then taste and adjust with more salt and pepper as needed.
* Serve hot.
