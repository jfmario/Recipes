# Egg Fried Rice

This is a fast, high-heat fried rice that uses pre-cooked, chilled basmati rice as the base component.

For best texture, cook the rice ahead and refrigerate it for at least 4 hours so the grains dry slightly and stay separate in the pan.

## Ingredients

* 8 cups cooked white basmati rice, chilled (use the `White Basmati Rice` component recipe)
* 8 large eggs
* 24 oz frozen mixed vegetables
* 1 medium yellow onion
* 6 garlic cloves
* 1 tbsp fresh ginger, finely grated
* 6 green onions
* 6 tbsp soy sauce
* 4 tbsp sesame oil
* 4 tbsp neutral oil

## Steps

* If needed, cook white basmati rice, then spread out 8 cups and refrigerate it for at least 4 hours (or overnight) before frying.
* Dice 1 medium yellow onion, mince 6 garlic cloves, grate 1 tbsp fresh ginger, and thinly slice 6 green onions; keep the green onions separate for finishing.
* Crack 8 eggs into a bowl and whisk until fully combined.
* Heat a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat, then add 2 tbsp sesame oil and 2 tbsp neutral oil.
* Add 12 oz frozen mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2 minutes until hot and no longer icy.
* Add half the onion, half the garlic, and 1 1/2 tsp of the grated ginger, then stir-fry for 1 minute until fragrant.
* Push the vegetables to one side, pour in 4 whisked eggs, and scramble them just until softly set, about 1 minute.
* Add 4 cups chilled cooked rice, 3 tbsp soy sauce, half the sliced green onions, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/4 tsp black pepper, then stir-fry for 2 to 3 minutes until the rice is hot and evenly seasoned.
* Transfer the first batch to a serving bowl and repeat with the remaining 2 tbsp sesame oil, 2 tbsp neutral oil, 12 oz frozen mixed vegetables, remaining onion, remaining garlic, remaining 1 1/2 tsp ginger, remaining 4 eggs, remaining 4 cups chilled rice, remaining 3 tbsp soy sauce, remaining green onions, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/4 tsp black pepper.
* Taste and adjust with up to 1 tbsp extra soy sauce, plus a pinch of salt and pepper if needed, then serve immediately.
