# Sweet Chili

This sweet chili is written for 8 servings and is designed to be hearty, kid-friendly, and easy to hold warm for serving.

Use lean ground beef and fully drain excess fat before seasoning so the finished chili stays rich without becoming greasy.

## Ingredients

* 1 1/2 lb lean ground beef
* 1 medium onion
* 2 garlic cloves
* 1 (16 oz) can baked beans
* 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
* 1 (15 oz) can black beans
* 1 (8.75 oz) can whole kernel corn
* Green onions, sliced, for garnish (optional)
* Shredded cheddar cheese, for garnish (optional)

## Steps

* Dice 1 medium onion and mince 2 garlic cloves. Open and drain 1 (15 oz) can black beans and 1 (8.75 oz) can whole kernel corn, then set both aside.
* Heat a medium Dutch oven over medium-high heat for 1 minute, add 1 1/2 lb lean ground beef, and cook for about 5 minutes, breaking it into small crumbles.
* Drain excess fat from the beef, return the pot to medium heat, add the diced onion, and cook for about 4 minutes until the onion turns translucent and the beef is fully browned.
* Add the minced 2 garlic cloves and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly, until fragrant.
* Add 3 tbsp brown sugar, 2 tbsp chili powder, 1 tsp paprika, 1 tsp fine sea salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper, 1/2 tsp onion powder, and 1/2 tsp ground cumin, then stir for 30 seconds to coat the beef evenly.
* Add 1 (16 oz) can baked beans, 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce, the drained 1 (15 oz) can black beans, and the drained 1 (8.75 oz) can corn, then stir until fully combined.
* Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer the chili uncovered for 30 minutes, stirring every 5 to 10 minutes so the bottom does not scorch.
* Taste and adjust with an additional pinch of salt or black pepper if needed, then serve hot with optional sliced green onions and shredded cheddar cheese.
