# Persian Kabob

Tender sirloin cubes soaked overnight in a savory, aromatic marinade then grilled alongside colorful vegetables. The soy-lemon-cardamom base is a Middle Eastern classic — simple to put together and deeply flavorful.

**Start the night before:** the beef needs at least 8 hours in the marinade for best results. Soak wooden skewers in water for 30 minutes before grilling if you're not using metal ones.

## Ingredients

* 2 3/4 lb sirloin steak, cut into 1-inch cubes
* 1/2 cup soy sauce
* 1/2 cup olive oil
* 3 tbsp plain yogurt
* 3 tbsp fresh lemon juice (about 1-2 lemons)
* 1 1/2 tbsp white vinegar
* 1 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
* 2 garlic cloves, minced
* 2 tsp yellow mustard
* 2 bell peppers, cut into 1-inch chunks
* 1 medium red onion, cut into 1-inch chunks
* 11 small mushroom caps
* 1 cup cherry tomatoes

## Steps

### The Night Before

* In a bowl or large measuring cup, whisk together 1/2 cup soy sauce, 1/2 cup olive oil, 3 tbsp plain yogurt, 3 tbsp lemon juice, 1 1/2 tbsp white vinegar, 1 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce, 2 minced garlic cloves, 2 tsp yellow mustard, 1 1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper, 1 1/2 tsp ground cardamom, and 3/4 tsp ground cinnamon until fully combined.
* Pour about 3/4 cup of the marinade into a separate container or small zip-lock bag and refrigerate -- this will be used for the vegetables.
* Add the 2 3/4 lb sirloin cubes to the remaining marinade in a large zip-lock bag. Press out as much air as possible, seal, and refrigerate overnight (8-12 hours).

### Day Of

* About 1 1/2 hours before you plan to grill, mince 2 garlic cloves, cut 2 bell peppers into 1-inch chunks, and cut the red onion into 1-inch chunks.
* Add the bell peppers, onion, 11 mushroom caps, and 1 cup cherry tomatoes to the reserved marinade bag. Seal and let sit at room temperature for 1 hour.
* Remove the beef from the refrigerator 30 minutes before grilling to take the chill off. If using wooden skewers, place them in water now to soak.
* Preheat your grill to medium-high heat (or oven to 400°F if cooking indoors).
* Thread the beef cubes onto skewers, leaving a small gap between pieces.
* Thread the vegetables onto separate skewers, grouping similar items together since peppers and onions take longer than tomatoes and mushrooms.

### Grilling

* Grill beef skewers over medium-high heat. Cook approximately 8-10 minutes for medium-rare, 12-15 minutes for medium, turning once or twice for even browning.
* Grill vegetable skewers alongside the meat, turning occasionally, until peppers are slightly charred and mushrooms are tender -- about 10-12 minutes. Add tomato skewers for the last 5 minutes only to prevent them from bursting.

### Oven Method (if not grilling)

* Arrange skewers on a foil-lined baking sheet. Roast at 400°F: 8 minutes for medium-rare, 13 minutes for medium, 18 minutes for medium-well, or 28-35 minutes for well-done. Rotate skewers halfway through.
