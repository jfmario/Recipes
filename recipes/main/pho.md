# Pho

Fragrant Vietnamese beef noodle soup with a long-simmered bone broth, brisket, and thin-sliced beef.

This version is scaled for 8 servings. Plan for a long simmer so the broth develops full flavor before serving.

## Ingredients

* 3 lb beef brisket
* 2 lb meaty beef bones
* 2 lb beef marrow bones
* 1 lb beef tenderloin
* 24 oz dried rice pho noodles
* 2 large yellow onions
* 6-inch piece fresh ginger
* 8 limes
* 4 red chilis
* 4 cups bean sprouts
* 2 cups Thai basil leaves

## Steps

### Build the Broth

* Set a large stockpot over high heat. Rinse 3 lb beef brisket, 2 lb meaty beef bones, and 2 lb marrow bones under cold water, then add them to the pot and cover with cold water by about 2 inches.
* Bring the pot to a full boil and boil for 5 minutes, then drain and rinse the brisket and bones to remove impurities. Rinse the pot, return the meat and bones, and refill with fresh cold water to cover by about 2 inches.
* While the pot heats again, place a dry skillet over high heat until smoking. Quarter 2 large yellow onions and halve a 6-inch piece of ginger lengthwise, then char both cut-side down for 3 to 5 minutes until deeply browned.
* Add the charred onions and ginger to the stockpot with 10 star anise, 4 cinnamon sticks, 4 cardamom pods, 3 whole cloves, 1 1/2 tbsp coriander seeds, 2 tbsp sugar, and 1 tbsp salt.
* Bring back to a gentle simmer, cover, and cook for 3 hours, skimming any foam as needed.
* Uncover and continue simmering for 40 minutes to concentrate flavor.
* Remove and reserve the brisket. Strain the broth through a fine-mesh strainer into a clean pot, discarding solids.
* Season the strained broth to taste with fish sauce, plus extra sugar and salt as needed. Keep the broth hot over low heat.

### Prep Meat and Garnishes

* Place 1 lb beef tenderloin in the freezer for 30 minutes so it firms up, then slice it as thinly as possible.
* Slice the reserved cooked brisket from the broth into bite-size pieces.
* Thinly slice 4 red chilis, cut 8 limes into wedges, and set out 4 cups bean sprouts and 2 cups Thai basil leaves for serving.

### Cook Noodles and Assemble

* Cook 24 oz dried rice pho noodles according to package directions until just tender, then drain and rinse briefly with warm water to prevent sticking.
* Divide the noodles evenly among 8 bowls. Top each bowl with sliced cooked brisket and a portion of raw sliced tenderloin.
* Ladle very hot broth over each bowl, using enough broth to fully submerge the noodles and gently cook the tenderloin.
* Serve immediately with lime wedges, sliced chilis, bean sprouts, Thai basil, hoisin sauce, and sriracha at the table.
