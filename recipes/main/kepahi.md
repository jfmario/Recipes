# Kepahi

These grilled sausages are best when the meat mixture rests before shaping, so plan Stage 1 at least 2 hours ahead (or the night before). Serve hot with the yogurt sauce and paprika-pepper finish.

**Start ahead:** Complete Stage 1 at least 2 hours before grilling.

## Ingredients

* 2 lb lean ground beef
* 1 lb lean ground pork
* 1 lb ground lamb
* 1 large onion
* 12 garlic cloves
* 6 tbsp chopped parsley
* 1/2 cup warm water
* 1 cup plain yogurt
* 3 tbsp olive oil
* 2 tbsp lemon juice

## Steps

### Stage 1: Mix and Rest the Meat

* Finely dice 1 large onion, mince 8 garlic cloves, and chop 6 tbsp parsley so all prep is ready before mixing.
* In a large bowl, combine 2 lb ground beef, 1 lb ground pork, and 1 lb ground lamb. Mix with your hands until the meats are evenly blended.
* Add the diced onion, 8 minced garlic cloves, 6 tbsp chopped parsley, 4 tsp paprika, 2 tsp cayenne pepper, 4 tsp red pepper flakes, 1 tsp baking soda, 4 tsp salt, 2 tsp black pepper, and 1/2 cup warm water.
* Mix thoroughly for 2-3 minutes until the seasonings and water are fully incorporated and the mixture feels slightly tacky.
* Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best flavor.

### Stage 2: Shape and Preheat

* Remove the meat from the refrigerator and divide it into 16 equal portions (about 4 oz each).
* Shape each portion into a sausage about 6 inches long and about 1 to 1 1/2 inches thick, pressing firmly so they hold together on the grill.
* Preheat a grill to medium-high heat (about 400-450F).

### Stage 3: Make the Yogurt Sauce and Spice Finish

* In a medium bowl, whisk together 1 cup plain yogurt, 3 tbsp olive oil, 4 minced garlic cloves, 2 tbsp lemon juice, and 1/2 tsp salt until smooth.
* In a small bowl, combine 1 tsp paprika and 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes for sprinkling at serving.

### Stage 4: Grill and Serve

* Oil the grill grates lightly, then place the shaped kebapi on the hot grill with a little space between each piece.
* Grill for 10-12 minutes total, turning every 2-3 minutes, until browned on all sides and cooked through.
* Transfer to a serving platter, sprinkle with the paprika-pepper mixture, and serve immediately with the yogurt-garlic sauce.
