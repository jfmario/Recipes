# Cajun Chicken Pasta

This is written for 8 servings and follows the one-pan method: chicken browns first, then onion, then dry pasta simmers in broth and tomatoes until tender, and cream cheese finishes the sauce. Use at least a 6-quart deep skillet or Dutch oven so everything fits and the pasta can simmer without sticking; stir often during the simmer.

If you want less heat, use 1/4 tsp cayenne in the spice mix instead of 1/2 tsp, or omit cayenne entirely.

## Ingredients

* 2 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts
* 2 medium yellow onions
* 1 lb dry penne pasta (or another short pasta shape)
* 2 (15 oz) cans fire roasted diced tomatoes
* 4 cups chicken broth
* 4 oz cream cheese (half a standard brick)
* 6 green onions

## Steps

* Dice 2 medium yellow onions. In a small bowl, stir together 4 tsp smoked paprika, 2 tsp dried oregano, 2 tsp dried thyme, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (use 1/4 tsp or omit for milder), 1/2 tsp black pepper, and 1/2 tsp salt to make the Cajun spice mix.
* Cut 2 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts into 1/2- to 3/4-inch cubes, place in a bowl, add the spice mix, and toss until the chicken is evenly coated.
* Set a large deep skillet or 6-quart Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add 2 tbsp olive oil and 2 tbsp butter and heat until the pan is very hot and the butter is melted and foamy.
* Add the seasoned chicken and cook for about 2 minutes per side until the pieces pick up color; they do not need to be fully cooked through yet.
* Add the diced onions and sauté about 2 minutes more until they begin to soften, scraping up any browned bits from the pan bottom with a wooden spoon.
* Add 1 lb dry penne, both cans of fire roasted diced tomatoes with their juices, and 4 cups chicken broth. Stir once to combine, then cover and bring the liquid to a boil.
* When boiling, reduce heat to medium-low and simmer about 12 to 15 minutes, uncovering to stir every couple of minutes, until the penne is tender and the liquid is thick and saucy; if the pan runs dry before the pasta is done, add a splash more broth or water and continue simmering.
* Cut 4 oz cream cheese into small chunks, add to the skillet, and stir until it melts completely into the sauce.
* Thinly slice 6 green onions, scatter over the pasta, and serve hot.
